Our Mission

Prevention is the new glow up! Our platform makes vaccine tracking simple, interactive, and empowering so users can confidently stay ahead of their health. It helps you see where you stand in your vaccination journey and shows what vaccines you may still need — because taking care of your health should be easy, clear, and stress-free.

The Two Girls Behind the Platform

What We Do

Welcome to the 3 W’s of Getting Immunized platform. Our goal is to help individuals stay informed and proactive about their vaccination health by making it easier to understand what vaccines they need, when they need them, and why they are important.Many people struggle to keep track of booster shots, updated vaccine recommendations, and age-based requirements, so we created this platform to organize that information in one simple and accessible place. By guiding users through personalized questions based on their age and health status, we aim to provide clearer insight into their immunization progress and any vaccines they may still need. We have a girly pop, design to make the design enjoyablable, and pretty duh! Additionally, we have a vaccination map, guiding users to nearby vaccination centers and clinics, making it easier to find where they can get vaccinated and levels so they move up! In addition to vaccination tracking, we also focus on women’s health awareness by including questions and information related to menstrual health, reproductive health, and preventive screenings. Understanding how hormonal changes, reproductive health, and overall wellness connect to medical care is important for long-term health. Our platform is designed to encourage awareness, education, and prevention so individuals feel more confident managing their health. Overall, our mission is to make health tracking more organized, understandable, and accessible for everyone. AS AN ADULT YOU ARE IN CHARGE OF YOUR HEALTH, AND WE WANT TO HELP YOU TAKE CONTROL OF IT!
